行内数学代码：$\sum\limits_{i=1}^ni=\frac{n(n+1)}2$

居中数学代码：

$$
\begin{aligned}&\sum_{k=1}^{n}1=n&&\sum_{k=1}^{n}k=\frac{n(n+1)}{2}\\&\sum_{k=1}^{n}k^{2}=\frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}&&\sum_{k=1}^{n}k^{3}=\frac{n^{2}(n+1)^{2}}{4}\end{aligned}
$$
